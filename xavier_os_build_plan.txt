Build plan

Phase 1
- Define product rules, content policy, and series bible.
- Design wireframes and navigation.
- Create a minimal listening flow.

Phase 2
- Build auth, catalog, chapter playback, and progression.
- Add AI chapter generation and TTS.
- Store chapter audio and metadata.

Phase 3
- Add perk fusion, recommendations, offline sync, and admin controls.
- Add moderation dashboards and analytics.

Phase 4
- Load test, fuzz test, regression test, and deployment hardening.
- Ship beta and iterate from feedback.
