The Blood Sword formed in his hand, now crackling with stolen celestial energy. One swing cleaved the warlord in half, his body disintegrating into cosmic dust. The army charged — and vanished into the void.

Quinn stared at the scroll. The path to godhood was clear. But the price... was everything.
